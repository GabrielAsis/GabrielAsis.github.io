Who are Santa Monica Studios?

Santa Monica Studio was founded by long-time Sony employee Allan Becker and originated in its namesake of Santa Monica, California; the company moved to Los Angeles in 2014. Over the years, Santa Monica Studio included several high-profile game developers among its ranks, including directors David Jaffe, Cory Barlog and Eric Williams, artist Stig Asmussen, writer Marianne Krawczyk, and producer Shannon Studstill.

Santa Monica Studio garnered acclaim for its work on the God of War series, with multiple individual titles receiving strong reviews and sales, and total series sales reaching over 50 million copies by 2020. Publications have praised the studio for its action sequences, production values, performance capture and scope present within their games. Their work on 2018's God of War and 2022's God of War Ragnarök attracted significant praise their drastic design changes and strong emphasis on narrative and characterization.

Characters

Meet the characters that live in the amazing world of Norse Mythology

Kratos

Kratos is a formidable and relentless warrior, bearing the weight of a troubled past. With a muscular build and piercing gaze, he exudes strength and determination. Clad in practical attire, his scarred skin tells the tale of countless battles fought. Kratos carries a solemn air, seldom revealing his emotions. Driven by a fierce sense of purpose, he embodies unwavering resolve and legendary combat skills. As he navigates his journey, he grapples with inner turmoil and seeks redemption, ultimately discovering the power of compassion and the importance of family.


Played by: Chritopher Judge

Atreus

Atreus is a young and curious companion on Kratos' epic journey. With a lean frame and bright eyes, he possesses an innocence that contrasts his father's solemn demeanor. Adorned in clothing befitting his Norse heritage, Atreus carries an air of vulnerability and untapped potential. He is a quick learner, displaying a sharp intellect and a growing understanding of the world around him. As the son of Kratos, Atreus is both a source of guidance and a reminder of the importance of humanity amidst the chaos. Throughout their odyssey, Atreus evolves from a sheltered youth to a capable and compassionate ally, proving his worth in the face of adversity.

Played by: Sunny Suljic

Freya

Freya, also known as the Witch of the Woods, is a complex and powerful character. With flowing golden hair and a regal demeanor, she radiates an ethereal beauty. Freya is deeply connected to the realms of Norse mythology and possesses formidable magical abilities. She is both a nurturing mother figure and a fierce warrior, fiercely protective of her son Baldur. Despite her initial antagonistic encounters with Kratos and Atreus, Freya demonstrates a depth of compassion and empathy, revealing a tragic past and a desire for love and redemption. Her journey is one of self-discovery and sacrifice, showcasing the duality of her nature as a loving mother and a formidable adversary.

Played by: Danielle Bisutti

Odin

Odin, the Allfather and ruler of Asgard, is a figure shrouded in mystery and power. With a long white beard and a single missing eye covered by a patch, he exudes an aura of wisdom and authority. Odin is known for his insatiable thirst for knowledge and his relentless pursuit of control over the realms. He is a master manipulator, orchestrating events behind the scenes to further his own agenda. Odin's presence instills fear and respect among both mortals and gods alike.

Played by: Richard Schiff

Thor

Thor, the God of Thunder, is a mighty and intimidating presence. With a massive physique and flowing red hair, he exudes an aura of raw power and aggression. Thor wields his enchanted hammer, Mjolnir, as his weapon of choice, capable of devastating displays of lightning and thunder. Known for his quick temper and belligerent nature, Thor is a formidable adversary and a symbol of Asgardian might. His relentless pursuit of justice often veers into brutal ruthlessness, leaving a path of destruction in his wake. As a key figure in Norse mythology, Thor represents the primal forces of thunder and lightning, embodying the chaotic and awe-inspiring aspects of nature.

Played by: Ryan Hurst

Tyr

Tyr, the Norse God of War and Justice, is a figure of wisdom and honor. With a regal and dignified presence, he exudes a sense of authority and serenity. Tyr is depicted as a tall and noble deity, often seen carrying a sword and a shield. He is known for his role as a peacemaker and mediator among the gods, seeking harmony and balance in the realms. Tyr's wisdom is reflected in his understanding of different cultures and his willingness to explore new lands. He is seen as a symbol of fairness and justice, revered for his diplomatic skills and his ability to navigate conflicts. Tyr's legacy serves as an inspiration and a guide for Kratos and Atreus on their own journey.

Played by: Ben Prendergast

Angraboda

Angrboða, a lesser-known character in Norse mythology, is a mysterious and enigmatic figure. With a striking and ethereal appearance, she possesses an otherworldly beauty and a certain air of danger. Angrboða is often associated with her connection to the Jotnar, the giants of Norse mythology, and is believed to be their mate. She is depicted as a powerful seeress and shapeshifter, with an innate connection to nature and the hidden realms.

Played by: Laya DeLeon Hayess
